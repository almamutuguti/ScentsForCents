OUR HISTORY

My journey with perfumes began when I was just in primary school, I used to talk about perfumes to my peers at school and when I came home from school, I would tell my parents all about it. During long holidays I would take my time and visit malls and browse through shops that sell original perfumes. I would ask the shop stewards about their sources however some would ignore and this would just make me feel discouraged.

I thought about how I would easily get access to networks effectively and at the comfort of my home. Whenever I sought for permission to access the internet from my parents or even access the gadgets, I would browse for perfume warehouses and send them emails by the help of my parents. After of doing that, I got responses from several perfumery warehouses.
