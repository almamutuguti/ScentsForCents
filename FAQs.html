The main difference is in fragrance concentration; Parfum (or extrait de parfum) has the highest concentration and lasts the longest. Eau de Parfum (EDP) is slightly lighter but still lasts 6 - 8 hours. Eau de Toilette (EDT) is lighter and ideal for a fresh, everyday scent.
